Hi! Thank you so much for downloading Life Simulator! 

To run the game, simply open home.html on your browser. This will bring you to the home page where you can begin your adventures in another life!

Life simulator uses cookies in order to save data into your computer. Local cookies are not supported on browsers like Chrome and Opera, hence it is impossible to run Life Simulator on these browsers. Therefore, life simulator recommends that you have Firefox Version 38 or later in order to run this game.
